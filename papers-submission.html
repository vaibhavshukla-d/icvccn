GUIDELINES TO AUTHORS

Paper Submission Guidelines

  1. Complete, unpublished papers should be submitted online in Adobe PDF format https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=icvccn1..
  2. Authors should anonymize manuscripts prior to submission. This entails removing or replacing author names and affiliations, acknowledgment, as well as any self-citations or URLs that would clearly disclose their identity. The paper should start from the title, abstract, keywords followed by the main paper.
  3. Papers exceeding plagiarism levels of 20% will be rejected in the screening process.
  4. Authors should submit their papers online in pdf format only and should not name the pdf file in their name. ICFMCF 2022 uses EasyChair for the submission of papers and the review process. Unregistered authors should first create an account on EasyChair to log in.
  5. Authors of accepted papers may be requested to act as discussants for other papers.

The paper submission is closed now.

EasyChair Submission Process:

  1. First participants need to sign up in EasyChair.
  2. Then the participant will receive EasyChair account confirmation email in provided email address.
  3. After this, the participant needs to fill up detailed information on the EasyChair account setup page along with the preferred user name and password.
  4. Now your account is created on EasyChair.
  5. Now login into EasyChair using your username and password.
  6. Click into conference link:  https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=icvccn1.
  7. Then click on "enter as an author"
  8. Fill in the details and submit your paper.

Publication opportunities of some selected papers accepted for the conference are being explored with suitable journals. Details will be posted when finalized.

Mode of Conference
